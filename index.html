Answers to questions

  • I'm going to invest and I want to know where to start?

    Start by defining your financial goals and investment deadlines. Study basic investment instruments such as stocks, bonds, and funds. Define determine your risk level and choose the appropriate investment portfolio. It is also worth consider getting advice from a financial advisor. Don't forget about diversification and a long investment horizon.

  • There is a loan, but there is no money. What to do?

    If you realize that you cannot make payments on the loan on time and in full, you need to contact the bank immediately. You should not hide and hope that your debt will be forgiven and forgotten. Even if collectors don't call you or write from the bank, interest and fines are still accrued, the debt is growing, and the credit history is deteriorating.

  • What is a family financial plan?

    This is a long-term forecast of the family's financial flows. With it, you plan how much money you will earn over a certain period and how you will spend it.

  • What is forex?

    Forex (short for Foreign Exchange) is a currency exchange. The forex market is primarily considered to be the international currency market, the largest and most active financial market in the world. Its daily turnover exceeds $5 trillion. This is more than the turnover of all national stock markets combined.
